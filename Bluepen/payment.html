Payment Policy

Welcome to www.bluepen.co.in!

• All kinds of assignments and projects are confirmed by Bluepen and started working on by us only on receiving either full payment in advance or partial payment of at least 50% of the total order value.
• Only upon receiving the complete payment, the assignment would be sent or dispatched to you.
• We accept payment in UPI, Bank transfer, Wallet Transfer, and Cash.
• All assignments, projects undertaken by us are confirmed and thereby allotted to the writers only once the user has processed us the advance.
• Thus the deadline for us begins only once we receive the advance payment.

Non-technical
For Handwritten and copy-paste assignments, the charges begin from INR 7 per side of an A4 size sheet. The rates may vary if the deadline is short, if there are complicated diagrams to be drawn and the charges are different if they need to be done on a specific sheet of the university or college of the user. Additionally, extra charges apply if the hard copy is to be delivered to the student’s doorstep. The total charges will be clearly mentioned to the user and only once the user accepts the price quotation and processes an advance, the order for the assignment is placed.

3. PhD Assignment (Indian University) : 3.5 INR to 4.5 INR per word. May vary depending upon the complexity, urgency and/or if any additional data collection, analysis, or primary research is required.
4. Bachelor’s Assignment (Foreign University) : 1.5 INR to 1.7 INR per word. May vary depending upon the complexity, urgency, and/or if any additional data collection, analysis, or primary research is required.
5. Master’s Assignment (Foreign University) : 1.8 INR to 2.2 INR per word. May vary depending upon the complexity, urgency. And/or if any additional data collection, analysis or primary research is required.
