Intern Roles

Project Manager

Project managers are responsible for planning and overseeing projects to ensure they are completed in a timely fashion and within budget. Project managers plan and designate project resources, prepare budgets, monitor progress, and keep stakeholders and company executives informed the entire way.

Roles and Responsibilities :
● Communicate with clients. Understand their requirements and delegate it to a freelancer from our database as per the project requirements.
● Communicate with the freelancer to keep a check on the progress of the project. Convey the same to the client.
● All kinds of 'operations' tasks required by the company

Human Resources Management

Human resources manager supervises a company or organization's hiring process, from recruiting, interviewing, and hiring new staff. They help connect executives with employees, build an employer brand, improve employee engagement, build strategic talent resources plans.

Roles and Responsibilities :
● Understand the company's requirement for new talent(s)
● Scout and hunt for talent using various online portals and other methods
● Schedule interviews. Help founders manage and conduct them.
● Do the complete on-boarding procedure. Help the new recruits with all the legal and corporate formalities and documentation on behalf of the company

Business Development

These individuals generate new sales leads, negotiate client pricing, and forecast sales revenue, all to support one of the most important business development manager responsibilities — helping organizations maximize their profits.

Roles and Responsibilities :
● Setting goals, developing and making strategies and plans for business revenue
● Researching, planning, and implementing new target market initiatives
● Researching prospective leads and potential clients in target markets
● Pursuing leads and moving them through the sales cycle
● Setting goals for the business and developing strategies to meet those goals

Web Tech Support

Web developers design and build websites. They are typically responsible for the appearance, of the site and technical aspects, such as site speed and how much traffic the site can handle. Web developers may also create site content that requires technical features.

Roles and Responsibilities :
● Run and maintain website of bluepen
● constantly developing new features, and elements on the website. Solve the bugs, glitches and issues that may arise.
● trial and testing of new features, elements and functions of the website
